Program Events

Theme: Charity Run 2025

Events:

  1. Art Performance: This is the opening event with singers and bands performing to create a joyful and encouraging atmosphere for participants.
  2. Running Events of 3km, 5km, 10km: Participants will start running according to the registered distances.
  3. Food Area: Selling food and drinks to serve participants and visitors.
  4. Amusement Area: Organizing games and entertainment activities for children and adults.

Objective: The objective of the Charity Run 2025 event is to raise awareness for the Environmental Protection Organization and motivate people to improve their health.
