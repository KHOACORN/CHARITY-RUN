Rules and Instructions for Participants

  • Pre-Registration Required: All participants must register in advance and comply with the event rules.
  • Liability Agreement: Participants will be required to sign a liability agreement before participating.
  • Age Requirements: Participants must be at least 16 years old for the 10K race, and 12+ for the 5K race.
  • Health Declaration: All runners must declare they are in good health to participate.
  • Event Conduct: Respectful behavior towards other participants, volunteers, and staff is mandatory.

Registration and Payment Instructions

Follow these simple steps to secure your spot in Charity Run 2025:

1
Visit Website
Go to the official Charity Run 2025 event website
2
Choose Type
Select your participation type: Runner, Volunteer, or Sponsor
3
Fill Form
Complete all required information on the registration form
4
Make Payment
Select payment method and complete the secure transaction
5
Confirmation
Check your email for registration confirmation and race packet info

Payment Methods Accepted:

  • Bank Transfer (Recommended)
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Digital Wallets (Momo, ZaloPay, ViettelPay)
  • Cash payment at designated locations

Important Deadlines:

  • Early Bird: Until September 15, 2025 (10% discount)
  • Regular Registration: Until October 1, 2025
  • Late Registration: Until October 8, 2025 (+20% fee)
