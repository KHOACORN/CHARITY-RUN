About Us

🏃‍♀️ Who We Are

Charity Run 2025 is more than just a running event – we're a community-driven organization dedicated to making a positive impact through the power of sport. Founded in 2020, we believe that every step counts, both in running and in creating meaningful change in our communities.

Our Mission

To bring people together through running while raising funds and awareness for important charitable causes that make a real difference in people's lives.

Our Vision

A world where fitness and philanthropy unite to create stronger, healthier communities that support those in need.
